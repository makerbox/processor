Student Results Processor

How to use:

  1. Choose start and end date (leave blank for all time) to filter by due date.
  2. Choose your CSV.
  3. Optionally, click "MATCH" to match your CSV columns with those the process uses.
  4. Click "PROCESS RESULTS".

View:

Match your columns

  • Drag your column names from the left onto the matching names on the right.
  • This ensures the processor knows what each of your columns are meant to be, regardless of their names in your CSV.
  • Any unmatched rows will just revert to the required heading, so you don't have to match all of them.
  • You can undo a match by clicking on it.
  • Click "SAVE MATCH" when you're done.
